## Content Scrambling System
- Is designed to be implented in hardware
- Linear feedback shift register
- Seed is the initial state of LFSR
- Used in DVD {CSS} (2 LFSRs), GSM {A5/1,2} (3 LFSRs), Bluetooth {E0} (4 LFSRs)
